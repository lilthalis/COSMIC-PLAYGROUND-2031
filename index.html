About the Cosmic Playground Festival

Cosmic Playground is a multidimensional music and art festival inspired by retro aesthetics, cosmic imagination, and surreal carnival atmospheres. Blending vintage textures with futuristic soundscapes, the event invites dreamers, creators, and explorers into an immersive world of mystery and celebration.

The festival takes place in a fictional universe called "The Void Garden," where cosmic attractions, star-lit pathways, and vortex gates guide visitors through an unforgettable artistic journey. Every detail is crafted to feel like a mix of an old vinyl poster, a forgotten amusement park, and a sci-fi dream.
